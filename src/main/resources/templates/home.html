Welcome to UH Groupings

Manage your groups in one place, use them in many places.

If you manage lists of people so that you send them email, or provide them privileges or permissions, or for other purposes, you can use UH Groupings to manage your lists in one place. Additionally, you can make use of groups that already existing in our Group Store.

For more information on UH Groupings and the Group Store, visit <https://www.hawaii.edu/bwiki/display/UHIAM/UH+Groupings>
