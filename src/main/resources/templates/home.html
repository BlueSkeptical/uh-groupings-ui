Welcome to UH Groupings

Manage your groups in one place, use them in many places.

UH Groupings allows you to create groups, manage group memberships, control members' self-service options, and more. Groups can be utilized as one or more of the following: email lists, permissions and privilege assignments for access control via CAS, etc. Additionally, UH Groupings allows you to leverage existing, automatically created and updated, groups, which can substantially reduce the manual overhead of group management.

For more information, visit <https://www.hawaii.edu/bwiki/display/UHIAM/UH+Groupings>
